Background benefits of Skipping Rope

Child development is best when kids get to play together at the playground.

Skipping rope is not only fun to play but is also a form of exercise.

I remember skipping my lunch meal after school just so I can get to play with other kids.

Here are some few benefits listed below;

  • It improves motor skills
  • Makes your muscles firm and fexible
  • Improves social interactions
  • Helps you to reset which is a good mental health practice
  • All in all skipping rope makes us happier as a kiddies game

Disadvantages of skipping rope

  • Kids can strain their muscles when they play
  • Knee injuries may also lead to a serious risk of stressed ankles
  • This activity may lead to fatigue and kids may struggle on their daily routines

Studies have shown that kids tend to have knee problems as they grow due to intensive activities they tend to play.
